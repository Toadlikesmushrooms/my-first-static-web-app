Shore city

Shore City is a premium shopping center located in Takapuna, Auckland, New Zealand . It offers a wide range of shopping options, including fashion, fresh food, takeaway food, health, beauty, and casual dining, as well as everyday essentials for the home and whole family . The center provides over 60 stores, fast free WiFi, and 3 hours of free car parking . There are also several events and offers available at Shore City, such as Santa's Shore Way Breakfast, Specsavers Spend and Receive, and Pet Photos with Santa .

The PumpHouse

The PumpHouse Theatre is a venue for hire for the performing arts and staged events in Killarney Park on the shore of Lake Pupuke in Takapuna, Auckland . It is a beautiful heritage building that was formerly a water pumping station that supplied Lake Pupuke water to North Shore’s early settlers . The theatre has a varied event calendar that includes annual Summer Shakespeare in the Park, adult drama, musicals, and theatre for children . The theatre is home to an array of theatrical performances, creative talks, comedy acts, and more . The facilities include a cafe, seating areas, and toilets . The theatre is accessible by car or public transport .

Thrillzone

Thrillzone is a one-stop venue for adrenaline-fueled indoor and outdoor activities in New Zealand. They have locations in Auckland’s CBD, Takapuna, and Queenstown . Thrillzone offers a wide range of exciting attractions, including the country’s only state-of-the-art Omni-VR room, escape rooms, laser tag, paintball, and more . They also have over 40 VR headsets, making them the biggest VR place in New Zealand . Thrillzone is perfect for all ages and occasions, from birthday parties to team building exercises . They offer a variety of group functions, including stag or hen parties, KidzClub, and team building .
